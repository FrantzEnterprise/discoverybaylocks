About Discovery Locks and More

Locally owned and operated locksmith serving Discovery Bay for over 20 years.

Our Story

Founded in 2005, Discovery Locks and More is owned and operated by local Discovery Bay residents Megan and Jerrod Moore. What started as a small family business has grown into the community's most trusted locksmith service, serving thousands of satisfied customers across the East Bay.

We continue to serve our hometown of Discovery Bay along with the surrounding areas including Brentwood, Oakley, Antioch, Pittsburg, Concord, San Ramon, Danville, Pleasanton, Livermore and more! Now offering services in the Central Valley as well!
