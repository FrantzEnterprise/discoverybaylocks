### **1. The "It Can't Happen to Me" Delusion**

**The Statistics:** 1 in 3 Discovery Bay residents will experience a lockout in the next 12 months. The most common scenarios:

  • **33%** - Keys left inside
  • **25%** - Lost keys
  • **20%** - Broken keys in locks
  • **15%** - Malfunctioning smart locks
  • **7%** - Other (child locks self, pet triggers lock, etc.)

    • **Discovery Bay Specific:** Waterfront homes add unique risks—keys dropped in the water, boat cabin lockouts, dock gate issues.

    **The Reality:** Lockouts don't discriminate by age, intelligence, or preparedness. They happen to everyone.
