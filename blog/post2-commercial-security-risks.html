Are You Putting Your Business at Risk? Commercial Security in Discovery Bay

June 30, 2026 • Discovery Locks and More

🏢 When "Good Enough" Security Isn't Good Enough

Running a business in Discovery Bay comes with unique challenges—and unique security risks. From waterfront restaurants to professional offices, we've seen how small security oversights can lead to major losses. Here are the commercial security risks most Discovery Bay businesses overlook until it's too late.

### **1. The "Trusted Employee" Access Problem**

**The Reality:** 75% of workplace thefts are committed by employees. Your business has turnover—employees leave, get fired, or change roles. But do their keys and access codes leave with them?

**Discovery Bay Business Example:** A local restaurant changed chefs three times in one year. Each chef had keys to the building, walk-in freezers, and liquor storage. When inventory started disappearing, they realized they had no idea how many keys were floating around.

**The Solution:** **Commercial rekeying or access control systems.** When an employee leaves, we can rekey affected areas for a fraction of replacement cost. Or better yet, install **keyless entry with individual codes** that can be deleted instantly.

### **2. The After-Hours Vulnerability Window**

**The Problem:** Your business closes at 5 PM, but cleaning crews, delivery drivers, and maintenance staff come and go at all hours. Each person with after-hours access is a potential security risk.

**Local Insight:** Discovery Bay's seasonal businesses (marinas, boat rentals, waterfront cafes) often have irregular hours and multiple part-time staff, creating complex access challenges.

**The Solution:** **Time-restricted access codes** or **audit trail locks**. These systems record exactly who entered, when, and which door they used. No more guessing who was in your business at 2 AM.

### **3. The "Secure" Storage That Isn't**

**The Risk:** That locked office supply closet? The "secure" file room? Most interior locks in commercial buildings are easily bypassed with simple tools. We're talking about locks that cost $15 to replace but protect $15,000 in equipment or sensitive data.

**True Story:** A Discovery Bay medical office had a locked narcotics cabinet secured with a basic cam lock. A temporary employee opened it with a paperclip in 30 seconds. The office didn't discover the theft until their monthly audit.

**The Solution:** **High-security interior locks** for sensitive areas. These cost slightly more but provide actual protection rather than just the illusion of security.

### **4. The Insurance Gap You Don't Know About**

**The Shock:** Many business insurance policies have **security requirements** that void coverage if not met. Your policy might require specific lock grades, alarm systems, or safe ratings that you're not aware of.

**Discovery Bay Specific:** Waterfront businesses often have additional insurance requirements for flood zones or marine environments that affect security system specifications.

**The Solution:** **Security compliance audit.** We'll review your insurance requirements and ensure your security measures meet or exceed them. This protects both your assets and your insurance coverage.
