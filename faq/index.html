Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our locksmith services in Discovery Bay, CA.

Do you offer 24/7 emergency locksmith service? +

Yes! Discovery Locks and More offers 24/7 emergency lockout service for residential, commercial, and automotive needs in Discovery Bay and surrounding areas. Whether you're locked out of your home, office, or car, call 925-634-0980 anytime — day or night, weekends and holidays included.

How much does it cost to rekey locks in Discovery Bay? +

Rekeying costs vary depending on the number of locks and the type of lock. We offer competitive, transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Call 925-634-0980 for a free estimate tailored to your specific needs.

Absolutely! We install all major smart lock brands including August, Schlage Encode, Yale Assure, and Kwikset Halo. Our team can help you choose the best smart lock for your needs and handle full installation and setup.

Can you program car key fobs? +

Yes! We specialize in car key programming, key fob replacement, transponder key cutting, and remote programming for all makes and models. We can help with both new vehicles and older cars.
